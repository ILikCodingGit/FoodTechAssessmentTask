5. Cooking Assessment – Luqi's Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or fettuccine – 1 handful
  • Bacon – 5 pieces
  • Parmigiano Reggiano – 200 ml
  • Pecorino Romano – 300 ml
  • 3 egg yolks
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Boil 1 litre of salted water.
  2. Cook pasta 10 minutes or until al dente.
  3. Dice the bacon.
  4. Place bacon in a cold pan, then heat and crisp it.
  5. Mix cheeses + yolks into a paste.
  6. Add pepper to the sauce.
  7. Add pasta to pan with heat OFF.
  8. Add sauce paste + pasta water to emulsify.
  9. Add more water gradually until creamy.
  10. Plate with extra bacon & pepper.

Cooking Skills Used

Boiling, frying, mixing, knife skills, timing, heat control.

Cultural Significance

Carbonara is an Italian pasta dish traditionally using guanciale. Bacon was used as a practical substitute.

Safety Risks

Risk of burns from boiling water and hot oil. Managed by careful handling and turning off the heat before adding eggs.

Sustainability

Ingredients are locally available and commonly produced in Australia.

What Went Well

The sauce turned creamy and smooth without scrambling.

Improvements

Use guanciale if available, and improve plating. Could also lightly toast a pecorino-egg topping for extra flavour.
