1. Identifying and Defining

Use your skills in horticulture to design and grow a successful vegetable garden suitable for the climate and space available at school.

2. Research and Planning

Research your plant and your partner’s plant — include where it’s grown, varieties, and care requirements.

3. Producing and Implementing

Document each stage: sowing, watering, transplanting, pest control, and harvest. Include images and reflections on process and safety.

4. Testing and Evaluating

Reflect on what worked, what didn’t, and what you learned about sustainable horticulture.

5. Cooking Assessment

Insert your recipe, image, and evaluation — include cultural significance, sustainability, and skills demonstrated.
